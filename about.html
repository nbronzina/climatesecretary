About the Secretariat

The **Climate Resilience Secretariat** leads Madrid's efforts to adapt to climate challenges like heatwaves, droughts, and floods. Our mission is to protect citizens, ensure sustainability, and make Madrid a global model for climate resilience.

Through **innovation, collaboration, and citizen-focused initiatives**, we design programs that reduce risks and prepare communities for extreme weather events. We prioritize transparency, accessibility, and data-driven solutions.

Meet Our Leadership

Isabel Sánchez

Isabel Sánchez, Chief Climate Officer, leads Madrid's climate adaptation strategy. With over 20 years of experience in environmental policy, she combines innovation, technology, and citizen engagement to build a more resilient city for future generations.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Open data and clear communication for all citizens.
  • Innovation: Leading with technology and creative solutions.
  • Community Focus: Empowering people to adapt and respond to climate challenges.
  • Equity: Supporting vulnerable communities to ensure no one is left behind.

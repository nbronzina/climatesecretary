About the Secretariat

The **Climate Resilience Secretariat** leads Madrid's response to climate challenges, ensuring the safety of its citizens, infrastructure, and environment. We build a sustainable future for everyone through innovative, data-driven solutions and community engagement.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

To design and implement solutions that protect Madrid from heatwaves, droughts, and floods while fostering sustainability and community preparedness.

Our Vision

To make Madrid a global leader in climate resilience, where technology, innovation, and citizen participation create a safe and sustainable city for future generations.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Open and accessible communication for all citizens.
  • Innovation: Adopting advanced technology to address climate challenges.
  • Community Empowerment: Ensuring citizens play an active role in resilience efforts.
  • Equity: Supporting vulnerable communities and leaving no one behind.

Meet Our Leadership

Isabel Sánchez

Isabel Sánchez is Madrid's Chief Climate Officer with over 20 years of experience in environmental policy and urban sustainability. She holds a PhD in Environmental Engineering and has led transformative projects across Europe and Latin America.

Isabel’s leadership combines technological innovation, policy development, and citizen-centered strategies. She firmly believes in collaboration, ensuring public and private sectors work together to build a resilient Madrid. Her efforts focus on reducing climate risks, fostering green infrastructure, and empowering communities to face future challenges.

The Secretary of Climate Resilience Initiative

Secretary of Climate Resilience: Pioneering Madrid's Path to a Sustainable Future.

Madrid has emerged as a global leader in urban climate resilience, setting a benchmark for cities worldwide. The transformation began with the establishment of the Secretary of Climate Resilience, a groundbreaking governmental office dedicated to addressing the challenges posed by the Urban Heat Island effect, prolonged droughts, and other climate-related stressors.

Emergency Response

The Secretary coordinates responses to extreme climate events, including floods, heat waves, and snowstorms.

  • Refuge Network: Shelters for cooling, warmth, or flooding safety.
  • Alert Systems: Real-time notifications and guidance for safety.
  • Resource Distribution: Water, portable cooling, and emergency supplies.
